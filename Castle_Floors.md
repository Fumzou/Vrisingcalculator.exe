# Castle Flooring

Castle FlooringDiamondSquareDiagonalEboniteBrick StablesWooden StablesRoyal *DLCPlague Sanctum *DLCAncient Symphony *DLCNocturnal Opulence *DLCRoomCastleResources2Stone BrickUnlocked ByExpanding my Domain(Quest)
Castle Flooringis a basic defaultCastle Flooravailable in V Rising. It is required to fill a room with flooring for it to count as a confined castle room (as well as provide a roof), though it does not matter which type of Castle floor is used, unless you are attempting to build specific rooms for their bonuses to crafting and refinement.
If placed on a a higher floor, sides which aren't connected to other floor tiles or walls will generate a balcony railing.
It is available in 10 different patterns, of which the Royal Pattern is exclusive to theDracula's Relics PackDLC, Plague Sanctum Tiles are exclusive to theSinister Evolution PackDLC, the Ancient Symphony Pattern is exclusive to theLegacy of Castlevania Premium PackDLC, and the Nocturnal Opulence Tiles are exclusive to theEternal Dominance PackDLC.
