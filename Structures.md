# Ancestral Forge

Ancestral ForgeRoomForgeResources1Primal Blood Essence8Radium Alloy16Iron IngotUnlocked ByOctavian the Militia CaptainDescriptionA workstation used for restoring ancient shattered weapons.Dimensions1x1
## Overview[]
Merciless & SanguineWeaponscan be upgraded at the Ancestral Forge into Rare & EpicAncestral Weaponsrespectively. Crafting ancestral weapons requires anancestral weapon shard, the attributes of which are alwaysrandomly generated. Rare ancestrals can be further upgraded fromGear Level20, to 24, to 27.
All ancestral weapons are randomly infused with aSpell Schooldebuff as well, causing SecondWeapon Skill(Q) hits to inflictLeech/Ignite/Condemn/Weaken/Chill/Static.
## Perks[]
- In Confined Castle Room: +25% speed.- Has Matching Floor(Forge Floor): -25% material cost.
