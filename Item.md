# Items

This page lists all inventory items, reagents, consumables, etc. and their recipes. Item stacks can be split evenly with the middle mouse button, and they can be pinned in the UI. You can take all items from a Storage Container with the default key Q. Finally, Crafting & Refinement recipes can be disabled at each station.See Equipment for a list of equippable items, Waygates for teleportable items, and Vendors for buyable items.

### Throwable Potions (Exclusive to Oakveil Woodlands)[]

- Beauty Elixir - Polymorphs all targets in the area into Sheep. Is required to end Lucile's "Alchemic Madness" state.
- Liquid Fire - Sets the area and all targets entering it on fire. The burn makes Gold Golems vulnerable to attacks for the duration.
- Pixies Luck - Dramatically increases player movement speed.
- Potion of Dispel - Knocks targets back and dispels all effects on targets hit, including Lucile's "Gold Skin" invulnerability.
- Rejuvenation Juice - Applies a healing buff to targets in the area. Taking damage ends the effect.
